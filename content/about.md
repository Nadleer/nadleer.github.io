I'm **nadler** — a bug bounty hunter and penetration tester.

This blog is where I publish:

- **Findings & write-ups** — vulnerabilities I've found, disclosed responsibly after remediation or explicit authorization.
- **Techniques** — offensive-security methodology: recon, JS analysis, OAuth, IDOR/BOLA, SSRF, race conditions, and more.
- **Automation notes** — tooling and workflows I use to hunt at scale.

### Disclosure policy

Everything here is published in good faith. I only write about targets I was authorized to test (bug bounty scope or engagement), and I never disclose a finding before the affected party has fixed it or explicitly approved disclosure.
