This is where I publish my offensive-security work: bug bounty findings, exploit write-ups, and the techniques behind them.

A few ground rules I hold myself to:

1. **Responsible disclosure first.** Nothing goes public until the affected program has remediated the issue or explicitly authorized disclosure.
2. **Evidence over hype.** Every write-up shows the actual proof of concept and the real impact — no exaggerated severity, no theoretical hand-waving.
3. **Methodology you can reuse.** I care more about *how* a bug was found than the bug itself. Expect recon notes, decision trees, and tooling.
